Who we are

Small-team pet sitting in London

Pet Sitters Club is a small, dedicated pet sitting service in London. We offer calm, reliable care for cats and small dogs, with daily visits in your home or overnight stays at ours.

Every booking gets the same attention we’d give our own pets: feeding routines kept, litter trays tidy, medication on schedule, and a quick photo update so you know they’re fine while you’re away.
